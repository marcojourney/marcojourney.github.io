Chea Serey Becomes First Female NBC Governor

Published on: Tue, 03 Oct 2023 - 6:28 PM | Updated on: Wed, 15 Nov 2023 - 1:19 PM | ~ 2 min read

Chea Serey, deputy governor of the National Bank of Cambodia (NBC), has been appointed to become NBC governor, replacing her father Chea Chanto.

According to a royal decree signed on July 29, Serey was named as the new governor of the NBC. It added that Prime Minister Hun Sen is in charge of enforcing the Royal Decree, starting on the day of the appointment.

Serey, former deputy governor of the NBC, said on her Facebook page that being promoted to the new role is the most precious present for her and her family. She added that she commits to working hard to carry out government policies.
